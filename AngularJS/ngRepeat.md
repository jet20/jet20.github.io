- [x] ngRepeat用于数组

## ngRepeat用于数组

## ngRepeat用于对象
ng-repeat="(key,value) in obj"


## ngRepeat局部变量

- $first, $middle, $last(bool)
- $even（%2==0), $odd(%2==1)
